Welcome to My Book Management System!

My Book Management System allows you to manage your book collection efficiently. Here's what you can do:

  • Add new books to your collection.
  • View details of each book, including title, author, description, year, and creation/update date.
  • Edit existing book details.
  • Delete books from your collection.

To get started, navigate to the "All Books" page to view your existing collection or click on "Add Book" to add a new book.
